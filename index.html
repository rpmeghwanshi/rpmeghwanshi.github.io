Hi! I am a final-year (5th) Ph.D. student in the Electrical Engineering Department at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur , supervised by Dr. Amit Bhardwaj and Dr. Himanshu Kumar.

Research

I study human perception of force and multisensory integration for object interaction, combining psychophysics, computational modeling, and human-centered experimentation. My recent works explore:

  • Effect of Round-Trip Delay in TPTA (ACM SAP'26)
  • Multi-Dimensional Deadzone (ACM TAP'26)
  • Effect of Continuous Transition (IEEE WHC'25)
  • Effect of Force-rate (Exp. Brain Res.'25)
