Research Interests

Understanding human perception and discrimination of force stimuli.
Haptic Psychophysics

Perception-Aware Haptics

Brief Bio

I am a fifth-year Ph.D. student in the Electrical Engineering Department at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Jodhpur .

I am supervised by Dr. Amit Bhardwaj and Dr. Himanshu Kumar .

My research focuses on human kinesthetic force perception and psychophysics , investigating perceptual mechanisms and developing computational models for applications in teleoperation, human–robot interaction, and perception-aware haptic systems.
